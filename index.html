Philosophical Ethics Decision Game

Dilemma 1

You witness a friend stealing from a store. If you report them to the authorities, they will face serious consequences. What would you do?

Dilemma 2

You are a doctor with limited resources. Two patients need a life-saving organ transplant, but there is only one available organ. Who would you choose to save?

Dilemma 3

You are a CEO of a company. A new product your company developed has potential safety issues, but releasing it would generate significant profits. What would you do?

Dilemma 4

You find out that a coworker is spreading false rumors about another colleague. What action would you take?

Dilemma 5

You come across confidential information about a competitor that could give your company a significant advantage. What would you do?

Dilemma 6

You witness a group of colleagues bullying a coworker. What action would you take?

Dilemma 7

You are a judge presiding over a high-profile case involving a close friend. What would you do to ensure impartiality?

Dilemma 8

You discover that your company is engaged in unethical practices that could harm the environment. What would you do?

Dilemma 9

You are a teacher and discover that a student has cheated on a significant exam. What would you do?

Dilemma 10

You are a software developer and discover a serious security flaw in your company's software. What would you do?
